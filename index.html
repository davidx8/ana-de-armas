AboutWho is she?

Born on April 30, 1988, in Havana, Cuba.
Global Actress: Known for her roles in both Spanish and Hollywood cinema.
Starred in notable films like "Blade Runner 2049" and "Knives Out", earning her critical acclaim and a Golden Globe nomination.
Philanthropy: Passionate about using her platform to advocate for causes close to her heart.
Continuing to take on challenging roles and projects, remaining a prominent figure in cinema.

Notable Movies

Blade Runner 2049

"Blade Runner 2049" Visual Effects: De Armas's character, Joi, is a digital hologram for much of the film, requiring sophisticated visual effects to achieve the translucent and ethereal appearance that made her role so visually captivating.

Knives Out

Ana de Armas's Preparation: For "Knives Out," Ana de Armas worked closely with writer-director Rian Johnson to develop the depth of her character, Marta. Her performance was pivotal to the film's narrative twist and emotional impact.

Blonde

"Blonde" Transformation: For her role as Marilyn Monroe in "Blonde," Ana de Armas undertook extensive dialect training to perfect Monroe's iconic breathy voice, highlighting her commitment to authentically portraying the film legend.
